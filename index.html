What is this?

This is a based on from a post made by Quartz on 400 free Ivy League university courses. It's just that, a list, or table, of 400+ Ivy League Courses you can take online for free. I thought it'd be beneficial to put them all into a searchable database.

How can I make my own?

On top of recreating the post into a database, I wanted to also set this up as a resource to be used to teach people both basics of web development and the use of Google Drive as a database. You can read my post on how I made this and what to do for your own.
